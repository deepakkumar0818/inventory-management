Complete Inventory Workflow

1

Purchase Orders & Receiving

Complete procurement workflow from vendor selection to inventory receipt and payment processing

  • Vendor Management: Vendor registration, qualification, performance tracking, payment terms setup
  • Purchase Orders: PO creation, approval workflow, vendor communication, order tracking
  • Purchase Receives: Goods receipt verification, quality inspection, quantity validation, documentation
  • Bill Processing: Invoice matching, three-way matching (PO/Receipt/Invoice), approval routing
  • Payment Management: Payment scheduling, vendor payment processing, payment reconciliation
  • Expense Tracking: Direct expense recording, expense categorization, cost center allocation

Vendor Onboarding
PO Approval

2

Items & Inventory Management

Comprehensive item master data management, categorization, pricing strategies, and inventory control operations

  • Items Master Data: SKU creation, product specifications, descriptions, dimensions, weight, barcode management
  • Item Groups: Product categorization, hierarchical grouping, group-level pricing, reporting by category
  • Price Lists: Multiple pricing tiers, customer-specific pricing, volume discounts, promotional pricing
  • Inventory Adjustments: Stock corrections, cycle count adjustments, damage adjustments, shrinkage tracking
  • Stock Counts: Physical inventory counts, cycle counting, ABC analysis, count variance reporting
  • Transfer Orders: Inter-location transfers, warehouse-to-warehouse moves, transfer documentation

Sub-Processes:

SKU Management
Product Catalog
Pricing Rules
Stock Adjustments
Cycle Counting
Location Transfers
3

Sales Orders & Processing

End-to-end sales workflow from customer acquisition to order fulfillment and payment collection

  • Customer Management: Customer registration, credit limits, payment terms, customer hierarchy, contact management
  • Sales Orders: Order creation, order modification, order approval, order tracking, order status management
  • Retainer Invoices: Advance payment processing, retainer management, retainer application to invoices
  • Invoice Generation: Automated invoicing, invoice templates, tax calculations, invoice approval workflow
  • Sales Receipts: Cash sales processing, receipt printing, payment method tracking, receipt reconciliation
  • Payment Collection: Payment tracking, payment matching, payment reconciliation, collection management

Sub-Processes:

Customer Onboarding
Order Processing
Credit Management
Invoice Automation
Payment Processing
Sales Returns
4

Fulfillment Operations

Complete warehouse operations including order picking, packaging, shipping, and inventory transfers

  • Picklist Generation: Automated picklist creation, pick route optimization, batch picking, wave planning
  • Packages Management: Package configuration, packaging rules, package tracking, shipping labels
  • Shipments Processing: Carrier selection, shipping rates, tracking numbers, delivery confirmation
  • Transfer Orders: Inter-warehouse transfers, transfer documentation, transfer tracking, transfer reconciliation
  • Quality Control: Picking accuracy verification, packaging quality checks, shipment validation
  • Warehouse Optimization: Location optimization, pick path optimization, inventory slotting

Sub-Processes:

Pick Planning
Batch Processing
Packaging Rules
Carrier Integration
Transfer Management
Quality Assurance
5

Stock Management

Comprehensive inventory control including stock counting, adjustments, monitoring, and optimization

  • Stock Counts: Physical inventory counts, cycle counting schedules, ABC analysis, count variance analysis
  • Inventory Adjustments: Stock corrections, damage adjustments, shrinkage tracking, adjustment approvals
  • Transfer Orders: Inter-location transfers, warehouse-to-warehouse moves, transfer documentation, transfer reconciliation
  • Stock Level Monitoring: Real-time stock tracking, reorder point alerts, safety stock management
  • Demand Forecasting: Sales trend analysis, seasonal adjustments, demand planning, procurement planning
  • Inventory Valuation: Costing methods (FIFO, LIFO, Average), inventory valuation reports, cost analysis

Sub-Processes:

Cycle Counting
Variance Analysis
Adjustment Workflow
Transfer Processing
Demand Planning
Cost Management

Financial Management

Complete financial workflow including payments, credits, returns, and comprehensive financial reporting

  • Payments Received: Customer payment processing, payment matching, payment reconciliation, collection management
  • Payments Made: Vendor payment processing, payment scheduling, payment approval workflow, payment reconciliation
  • Sales Returns: Return authorization, return processing, return documentation, inventory restocking
  • Credit Notes: Credit note generation, credit application, credit approval workflow, credit tracking
  • Financial Reporting: P&L statements, balance sheets, cash flow reports, financial analytics
  • Tax Management: Tax calculations, tax reporting, tax compliance, multi-jurisdiction tax handling
